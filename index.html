DecentraStor: Blockchain-Powered Cloud Storage
with Keeper-and-Distributor Systems

As the Founder of DecentraStor, I'm excited to offer a blockchain-powered solution to help companies address their data storage issues. Today, businesses face a growing number of data risks and security threats, which is why I drew inspiration from FileCoin and Storj to develop a more secure and efficient solution for data storage and retrieval. DecentraStor utilizes private blockchains to offer enhanced cybersecurity through data "keepers and distributors", providing companies with a more optimized and secure way to access their data.

Bass Connections @ Duke:
Gamifying Risk Identification for Alcohol Use Behaviors Across Countries and Cultures

I am currently contributing as an app development team member to the creation of the Alcohol Use Behavioral Phenotyping Test (AUBPT), a gamified tool that assesses biopsychosocial constructs related to alcohol use. Our team expanded AUBPT's implementation globally, adapting it for Portuguese and Hindi use in Brazil and India. Using the National Institute of Mental Health's Research Domain Criteria framework, we tested AUBPT's ability to predict alcohol use disorders and created simulated AI agents to model patient performance.

My current work involves converting Unity-developed games into a WebApp version using WebGL

Cloud-based Framework for Real-time BCI
and IoT Analytics with TinyML Integration

I am contributing to the development of a cloud framework for real-time brain-computer interface (BCI) and Internet of Things (IoT) analytics using TinyML under the guidance of Prof. Ming-Chun Huang. Our project's goal is to integrate openBCI hardware with IoT devices and send electroencephalography (EEG) data to a STREAM websocket API and digital twin platform for real-time machine learning analysis. As part of the project, I collaborated with the University of Buffalo openBCI team and a student development team to explore the potential for real-time BCIs and to create a pipeline for machine learning analysis. We utilize the following stack and framework: STREAM Websocket, Digital twin platform, OpenBCI, TinyML, Postman (API), and AWS. Through this project, I hope to gain a valuable experience in developing cloud-based solutions for real-time BCI and IoT analytics, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and working with cutting-edge technologies in the field of machine learning.

HeritageXR

As a startup co-founder under the guidance of Professor Liqi Ren, I helped to create HeritageXR, which is a startup that aims to implement educational mobile apps with virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) capabilities. Our goal is to create an immersive and educational experiences for users to explore Vietnamese architectural structures and document the "way of life" of locals through 3D visualizations. I work with Blendr, UnityVR, and Adobe AERO for 3d visualization, and Flutter and AWS for the app dev. We are going to conduct on-site research and interviews with locals in central and southern Vietnam (Saigon) to gain a better understanding of Vietnamese culture, history, and architecture. The project is ongoing, and started in Spring 2023. I am hoping to gain valuable experience in startup co-founding, developing mobile apps with VR and AR integration, and conducting field research to create meaningful educational experiences
